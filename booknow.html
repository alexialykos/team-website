#1

Northern Ireland - Giant Causeway

Explore the basalt columns of Giant Causeway along the seacoast! Created by 60 million years of volcanic rock, they are now known for their rare beauty and hexagonal shape.

When to visit?

This dramatic sight waits for you to visit at any time of the year!

#2

Malta - Popeye Village

A small attraction village in Malta has a lot to offer. From wonderful views to fun activities, such as boat trips! Popeye Village has it all.

When to visit?

Do not worry, this enjoyable trip is open all year round!

#3

Mexico - Cancún

Come to Cancún, a tourist friendly and safe place to travel to place. With its dyring warm weather, Cancún can cool you off with its beautiful beaches. It offers a variety of activities, like scuba diving and surfing!

When to visit?

To have the best experience without many tourists, the best time to visit is during April to May and September to October.

#4

Tokyo - Japan

Come to visit Japan's national flower, the cherry blossoms! Not only are the flowers known for their beauty, but also for their symbolic meaning behind it.

But when is the ideal season for visit?

To not miss this extraordinary sightseeing, the last week of March until the first week of April is the perfect time for you.

#5

Austria - Innsbruck

Austria is famous for skiing and the best place to do that is in Innsbruck. Don't know how to ski? No problem. Innsbruck has resorts for everyone. From ski beginners to professional skiers.

When to visit?

For an Innsbruck ski vacation, the season typically runs from early/mid-December to March/April.

#6

Thailand - Bangkok

It is no coincidence that Bangkok is one of the most visited cities in the world. Maintaining its historical architecture and traditions, Bangkok gives the opportunity for tourists to visit its grand palaces and temples, gigantic skyscrapers, and so much more!

When to visit?

For a cool vacation, late November and early December are the best times to visit Bangkok.
